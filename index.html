Innovation Project

Car Stuck in Snow

GEN302 – Fundamentals of Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Sustainability

Introduction

Harsh winter weather creates serious challenges for drivers. Heavy snowfall, icy roads, and poor visibility often cause vehicles to become stuck in snow.

Solution 1 – Smart AI Snow Car

A vehicle engineered for snowy environments using AI sensors, adaptive tires, heating systems, and automated snow‑handling tools.

Solution 2 – Heated Sidewalk Snow Mattress

A heated ground system that prevents snow accumulation using sensors and salt spray.

Solution 3 – Automatic Protective Car Shield

A protective shell that covers parked cars and prevents snow buildup.
